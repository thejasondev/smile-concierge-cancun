## ğŸ’¡ CONTENT CAPTURE TIPS

### **For Photographers:**
1. **Golden Hour** - Shoot 1 hour after sunrise/before sunset
2. **Natural Poses** - Have subjects interact naturally
3. **Rule of Thirds** - Don't center everything
4. **Depth** - Use f/2.8-f/4 for background blur
5. **Candid First** - Capture real moments, posed second

### **For Patients:**
1. **Consent Forms** - Get written permission
2. **Incentivize** - Offer discount for photo rights
3. **Comfort** - Make them feel at ease
4. **Variety** - Multiple angles, expressions
5. **Context** - Show treatment + lifestyle

### **For Clinics:**
1. **Daytime** - Natural light through windows
2. **Clean Up** - Hide clutter, organize
3. **Activity** - Show team working
4. **Details** - Close-ups of equipment
5. **Wide Shots** - Establish space
